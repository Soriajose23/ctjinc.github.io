Synthetic Roofing Information

Carl T. Johnson Inc. can install synthetic roof tiles from the industries leading suppliers. These synthetic tiles are made from blends of new or recycled plastic that can last for decades. A proprietary molding process is used to create tiles in a wide variety of formats (Synthetic slate, shake, clay, etc...) that look better and are far stronger than the natural materials. They can be installed over asphalt shingle shingles or as tile roofs and for commercial applications.

Benefits

  • Can last for decades or more.
  • Aesthetic beauty
  • Eco-friendly
  • Lightweight, ease and speed of application
