Slate Roofing Information

Slate roofing tile has a long and storied history, and is generally known as one of the highest quality, longest lasting roofing materials on the market. But there are many things homeowners should be aware of before choosing slate roofing tile. Homeowners are drawn to slate tile mainly because of its beauty and longevity. Slate tile can come in varying thicknesses and natural mixed colors. When properly installed, slate tile is buit to last at least a century. On the other hand, slate tile can be very costly and it can be very hard to replace damaged tile. Homeowners also need to have their residences evaluated before installing slate tile to make sure their homes can support the weight.

Benefits

  • Comes in varying sizes and colors
  • Fire resistant
  • Built to last at least a century
  • Environmentally friendly
