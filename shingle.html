Asphalt Shingle Roofing Information

Home owners today have a wide selection of different roofing types to choose from with asphalt shingles being one of them. Asphalt shingles offer a beautiful, affordable, and reliable roofing solution for most residential homes. That's why 4 out of 5 homes today have asphalt shingles installed on their homes (Asphalt Roofing Manufacturers Association). Carl T. Johnson, Inc. uses industry products from manufacturers with a reputation for quality and durability like GAF, the largest roofing manufacturer in North America. We will work with you to choose the right type of shingle manufacturer, color, and texture for your home that fits your budget and goals.

Benefits

  • Wide variety of colors and styles (tile, cedar, shake, wood, slate). Gives home natural look
  • Relatively easy to install or repair, reducing materials needed.
  • Long life span.
  • Easy to maintain.
  • Low cost.
