Cedar Shake Roofing Information

All roofing materials have pros and cons associated with them, and cedar is no exception. Wood shake shingles give a natural look to your home and they are commonly made out of cedar, redwood, or cypyress. Cedar roofing is long lasting and can come in a wide variety of formats (shingle, shake, and hip & ridge). Cedar shake is also durable to the elements and can be treated with chromated copper arsenate to be fire-resistant, fungus-resistant, andd insect-resistant. Wood roofing is more costly to install than asphalt shingles. However, over time, the longevity of cedar wood roofing provides a higher return on investment. Regular maintaince should also be done on a wood roof.

Benefits

  • Eco-friendly roofing material
  • Can last ten years or longer than asphalt shingles
  • Provides natural insulation up to two times that of asphalt shingles
  • Natural beauty of wood
