Roof Coating Information

Roof coatings offer a cost-effective option to extend the life of your current roof. A roof coating is a fluid applied roofing membrane that becomes the top layer of an underlying system. Roof coatings can solve roof leaks on almost any type of roof material and adds a level of protection against the sun and weather. There are various types of coatings on the market, Carl T. Johnson Inc. can help you choose the best type of coating for your roof.
