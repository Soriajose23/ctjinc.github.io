Clay Roofing Information

Clay roofing dates back thousands of years. Clay roofs can be manufactured in a wide variety of colors, textures, and shapes to give a home an old world look and design. Spanish roof tile and southwestern roof tile are some of the most popular designs. Clay tiles are also manufactured in slate or shake designs. If properly installed, clay tiles are expected to last longer than any other material. Clay tiles are fragile, so replacing them can be difficult, since each tile is somewhat uniqure to each home.

Benefits

  • Long life cycle
  • Environmentally friendly
  • Extremely resistant to the elements
  • Wide variety of styles and colors
